About the Creator


Danny Melvin (Creator)


Danny Melvin grew up in Syracuse, NY and attended Christian Brothers Academy. At CBA he played soccer, basketball, and track as well as playing two years of baseball. He now attends Rollins College in Winter Park, FL as a rising junior and is majoring in economics with a double minor in computer science and data analytics. Danny is a diehard soccer fan and wants to make soccer into something he can turn into a job. The United Kingdom Football Exchange™ is a way to follow the love of the game and also make money off of your favorite teams when they do well.
